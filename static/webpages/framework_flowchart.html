Unified Investment Framework Flowchart

A detailed synthesis of the Lynch and O'Neil strategies, based on the "Architects of Alpha" report.

Step 1: Idea Generation (Lynch/Fisher Foundation)

The process begins with qualitative, real-world observation to build a list of potential investment ideas.

  • "Invest in What You Know": Identify companies from everyday life (favorite products, expanding local businesses).
  • "Scuttlebutt" Method: Investigate by gathering informal intelligence from sources like:
    • Customers (reviews, satisfaction)
    • Employees (morale, culture via Glassdoor)
    • Suppliers & Competitors
↓

Output: Initial List of Companies with a Compelling "Story"

↓

Step 3: Rigorous Vetting (O'Neil's CAN SLIM Filter)

Subject the curated list to a demanding quantitative filter to ensure the story is backed by the statistical DNA of a market winner.

↓

Check 'C' & 'A': Earnings Growth

  • Current Quarterly EPS Growth >= 25%
  • Current Quarterly Sales Growth >= 25%
  • Annual EPS Growth (3-5yr) >= 25%
  • Return on Equity (ROE) >= 17%
