Architects of Alpha

An interactive guide to synthesizing the master strategies of William J. O'Neil and Peter Lynch for superior stock selection.

The Arena: Philosophy Showdown

Both O'Neil and Lynch were titans of growth investing, but their methods were distinct. This section allows you to visually compare their core philosophies across key strategic pillars. Interact with the chart and text to understand their fundamental differences.

Trader vs. Owner

The most profound difference lies in their identities. O'Neil's rigid 7-8% stop-loss is the action of a trader managing price risk. Lynch's focus on a deteriorating fundamental story is the action of a long-term business owner managing business risk. Use the buttons to explore their approaches.
